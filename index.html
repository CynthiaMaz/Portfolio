Field of Study

My studies center on database theory and biological anthropology for the identification and repatriation of skeletal remains. By working in tandem with archival records and analysis of the remains, databases supporting the integration of both can help facilitate the identification process, allowing for a greater number of individuals to be returned to their families.

Extracurriculars

  • Archival Management

    I have participated in multiple efforts to reorganize and rehouse archival collections at Pitt and historical community collections.

  • Students Conquering Cold Cases

    I currently serve as the Vice President for SCCC, an organization that collaborates with the Allegheny DA's Office and a local detective to assist with current efforts to close cold cases in the Pittsburgh area.

  • Social Network Theory

    I am very interested in how social networks form and operate, and how this knowledge can be applied for Cold Case research or Identification of remains. I spend time researching this independently to integrate it into my studies.
